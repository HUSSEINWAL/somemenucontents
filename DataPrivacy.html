1. Overview

This Data Privacy Policy explains how PHOPCHAT collects, uses, protects, and manages your data when you use our social platform. Our goal is to give you transparency, control, and security over your personal information and content.

2. Types of Data We Handle

We process the following categories of user data to provide our services effectively:

  • Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, and profile details such as bio, profile picture, and gender.
  • Social Data: Posts, comments, likes, followers, friends, and user-generated content (UGC).
  • Device & Activity Data: Device type, operating system, IP address, and app usage analytics.
  • Media Permissions: Access to camera, microphone, and gallery (only when you record or upload content).

3. How We Protect Your Data

We apply multiple layers of security to ensure your personal and social data remain safe:

  • Encrypted data transfer (HTTPS/TLS) between your device and our servers.
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with restricted access controls.
  • End-to-end encryption for private chats or messages (if available).
  • Regular audits and vulnerability checks to maintain platform security.
  • Privacy-by-design principles in all new social features.

4. Social Interaction and Visibility

Since PHOPCHAT is a social platform, certain activities are naturally visible to others. However, you maintain full control over your privacy settings:

  • You can choose who sees your content (public, followers, or private).
  • You can block, report, or restrict users.
  • You can delete your posts, stories, or comments at any time.
  • Your private messages remain confidential and are not visible to other users.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality, maintain security, or comply with legal requirements.

  • Deleted accounts and content are typically removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Backup copies may persist briefly for recovery and safety before permanent deletion.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your stored personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw permissions for camera, location, or data access at any time.
  • Request a copy of your data for portability.
